Utah State University Marine Robotics Club

The Aggie Marine Robotics club is a team of students at USU studying Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Computer Science. We are building an autonomous submarine for the AUVcapable of navigating an obstacle course and performing various tasks, such as circling a buoy, launching a torpedo at targets, depositing a payload, surfacing within a ring, and locating a target with sonar.
